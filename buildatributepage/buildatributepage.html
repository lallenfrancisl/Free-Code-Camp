QUINCY LARSON

Quincy Larson is the guy who created FreeCodeCamp. It is a website which teaches people to program for free.
Quincy founded FreeCodeCamp.org, a community of millions of people who learn to code together - both on our learning platform and through in-person study groups in more than 2,000 cities around the world. Before that, he did his grad work in China through an American university, and went through an intensive Mandarin program. During his five years in China, he taught at and served as an interpreter for various language training operations. He also recruited MBA students. He worked as a teacher and tutor throughout college and graduate school. He served as director of several schools in the US and China, and increased their academic rigor while simultaneously growing their student populations. Along the way, he learned to code. Quincy wrote scripts to automate our school workflows so he could free teachers and administrators to spend more time with students.
